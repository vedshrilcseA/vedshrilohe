Tribute to Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is a famous Indian industrialist and former chairman of the Tata Group. He is known for his leadership, innovation, and contributions to the growth of Indian industries. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded globally and launched successful projects like the Tata Nano and Tata Motors expansion.
